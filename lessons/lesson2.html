Now, how about some color?

You can give twinkle some extra instructions.

Try this code out: 

twinkle (red: 255, green: 0, blue: 0)

What color do you see? Try changing those numbers to see what you get.

You can mix three colors of light - red, green, and blue - to get all the shades in between. red: 0 means the red light is all the way off. red: 255 means it is all the way on. Numbers in between 0 and 255 mean it's part of the way on.
